### Keyboard interactions

<KeyboardControls>
  <KeyboardControl keyOrCombos="Enter or Space">
    - When focus is on the pill, activates.
    - When focus is on the close button, closes the pill.
  </KeyboardControl>
  <KeyboardControl keyOrCombos="Tab">

    - Sets focus on the entire pill.
    - When the pill has focus, moves focus out of the pill to next component in tab order.
    - When a closable pill has focus, moves focus into the close button.

  </KeyboardControl>
  <KeyboardControl keyOrCombos="Shift + Tab">
    - When the pill has focus, moves focus out of the pill to previous component in tab
    order.
    - When the close button has focus, moves focus back to the pill.
  </KeyboardControl>
</KeyboardControls>
